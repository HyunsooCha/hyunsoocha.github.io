Durian
Dual Reference Image-Guided Portrait
Animation with Attribute Transfer

Hyunsoo Cha Byungjun Kim Hanbyul Joo

Seoul National University

Paper arXiv OpenReview Code (Coming Soon)

TL;DR — Given a portrait image and single or multiple reference images specifying target attributes (e.g., hairstyle, eyeglasses), Durian generates a portrait animation with facial attribute transfer conditioned on a keypoint sequence.

Demonstrations

Results

All result videos are complete zero-shot inference results not included in the training set, without any additional training or optimization. We present results of transferring attributes to the portrait while simultaneously animating it.
